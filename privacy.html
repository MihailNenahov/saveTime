Enki – Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-10-28

Enki does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What the extension does locally

  • Applies user-chosen rules to block or redirect visits using Chrome's declarativeNetRequest (main-frame only).
  • Optionally hides or rearranges interface elements on supported sites (e.g., YouTube, Instagram) by modifying the page DOM on the device.

Data storage

  • Stores only user settings (URL patterns and feature toggles) in chrome.storage.sync.
  • These values are simple strings/booleans. No browsing history, page content, or identifiers are stored by the developer.
  • If Chrome Sync is enabled, Google may sync these settings between the user's devices per Google's policies.

Network and third parties

  • No data is sent to the developer or third parties.
  • No analytics, tracking, or external servers.

Permissions

  • <all_urls> host access is required to evaluate user-specified rules and to apply on-page UI changes.
  • declarativeNetRequest is used to redirect matching navigations to the bundled redirect.html.
  • storage is used only for user settings.

Children

Not directed to children under 13.
